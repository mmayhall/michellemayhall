WHY


This hypothetical company, Saurus Chocolates, makes informative, delicious, and entertaining dinosaur-themed treats for children, intended to be sold in natural history museums and their online store. These bars provide fun, kid-friendly facts, and the inside of each wrapped bar doubles as a coloring page! Crayons included!
