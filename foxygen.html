Foxygen’s We Are the 21st Century Ambassadors of Peace and Magic

Album & Illustrated Lyric Booklet Design

⤓

WHAT


Record sleeve, record label, and illustrated lyric booklet for the band Foxygen’s album We Are the 21st Century Ambassadors of Peace and Magic

Download the lyric booklet pdf here



WHY


These items were created with the intention of being re-released as a promotion for Foxygen’s new album, Hang. Since Foxygen's influence comes from the sixties and seventies, themes from that time were incorporated into the design, while still staying modern.
