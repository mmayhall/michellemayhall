The Game of Chess

Instructional Book, Motion Graphic, & Packaging Design

⤓

WHAT


Instructional book on how to play chess, motion graphic explainer animation on how each piece moves, and board game packaging for The Game of Chess

Download the book here


Click above and use arrow keys, or drag across pages with your mouse to navigate through the book.



WHY


Inspired by Russian constructivism, this overall design, applied to each of the products, is intended to convey the mental intensity of chess through sharp geometry, high contrast, saturated color and intimidating angles.


HOW


Adobe Illustrator
Adobe After Effects
Adobe InDesign
