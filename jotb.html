WHAT


Book design for Loren Eisley's short story, The Judgement of the Birds

Download the pdf here


Click above and use arrow keys, or drag across pages with your mouse to navigate through the book.

WHY


The purpose of this book design was to convey the concept that since nature is ethereal, massive, and scary, it is often easier for humans to stay confined in the world they have created than to immerse themselves in nature. This is shown through the contrast of the bold type encased in a box and the very light type on the white paper.
