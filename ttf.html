WHAT


Sample spreads, cover, and character designs for "The Judgement of Paris," renamed "To the Fairest"

This graphic novel tells the tale of Peleus and Thetis' wedding, to which Eris — the goddess of discord — was not invited. Because of this offense, Eris drops a golden apple that reads "to the fairest" into the setting of the wedding. This leads to an argument between Aphrodite, Athena, and Hera, who all believe themselves to be the fairest, and therefore deserving of the apple. This argument, solved by Paris declaring Aphrodite the fairest in exchange for the love of Helen, leads to the Trojan War.

WHY


The goal of this project was to create innovative shape- and color-based character designs of Greek gods and goddesses, and incorporate those designs into a graphic novel format.
