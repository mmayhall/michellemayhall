commission request

Thank you for your interest in my work!


My commission process is pretty simple:

1) From the image below, please select a pricing option. My preferred payment method is Venmo, @Michelle-Mayhall. I also accept PayPal at paypal.me/mshellillustrates.


2) Upload your reference photo(s). I can replicate a photo, or create a new pose. Either is the same price, just indicate which you want.


When I receive the full payment, I'll get to work! I ask 2–3 weeks to create, though it may be less depending on how many commissions are queued.


When it's finished, I will email you a jpg of your piece. If you would like anything simple to be changed (such as clothing color) I can do that no problem! More significant changes will result in additional cost, since it requires additional time — so please be very descriptive about your vision.


To request your commission please fill out this form! Woo! Let's get started!
