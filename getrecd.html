Get Rec'd

Event Logo & Advertising Campaign

⤓

WHAT


Logo and social media motion graphics campaign for Auburn Campus Recreation's annual welcome week event Get Rec'd

Learn more about the event here


WHY


This logo was designed in a badge format to be all encompassing of the many aspects of this event. By incorporating the name, the "Ready. Set. Play." tagline, and the logo mark that alludes to both a person and a ball, this badge conveys Get Rec'd's combination of fitness and fun.
